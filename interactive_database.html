Methodology

Four-parameter weighted model. PhysShare weights the robot vs AI contribution per occupation. Barrier suppresses displacement even when technically feasible. Source code and full README at github.com.

Data sources

  • IFR World Robotics 2022 — robot density
  • Eloundou et al. 2023 — AI β-scores
  • Anthropic Economic Index 2026 — observed AI use
  • O*NET Work Activities 4.A.3.* — physical share
  • BLS licensing data 2023 + Pew surveys 2014/22

Validation

14 of 15 published anchors within ±10 points. Mean absolute error 4.7 pts. See README.md for full validation table and known limitations.
